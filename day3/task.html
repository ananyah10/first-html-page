About the picture

Members of our Your Shot photography community take us on a trip underwater through their camera lenses, not only into the worlds oceans but also to cenotes in Mexico, a river in Switzerland, and an Austrian lake. Along the way, we meet creatures both great and small, from humpback whales to a skeleton shrimp. The photographers let us peek into a different world but, as witnesses to climate change's affects on the oceans, also provide us with a glimpse into an uncertain future.

Photography

Photography is the art of capturing light with a camera, usually via a digital sensor or film, to create an image. With the right camera equipment, you can even photograph wavelengths of light invisible to the human eye, including UV, infrared, and radio.
